Before you order a digital certificate

You should check that a digital certificate with an electronic Companies House signature will be accepted by:

  • the person or organisation asking for the certificate (for example, the bank or embassy)
  • your notary, if you are getting the certificate notarised

The certificate will only be valid in digital form as it contains a unique electronic signature. If they need a paper certificate with a wet ink signature, you should order a paper certificate instead.

What is an electronic signature?

An electronic signature will not look like a wet ink signature. It will show the Companies House seal and the date that the document was embedded with the electronic signature.

You will need to open the PDF in Adobe reader to view the signature.

For now, digital certificates will not include a statement of good standing. If you need this, you should order a paper certificate instead.
