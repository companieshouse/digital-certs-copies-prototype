Before you order a digital certificate

You should contact the person asking for the certificate to check if they will accept a digital certificate with an electronic Companies House signature.

Digital certificates are only valid in digital form. You may need to order a paper certificate if the person who is asking for it needs:

  • to include a statement of good standing
  • a paper copy with a wet ink signature
