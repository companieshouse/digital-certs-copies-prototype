Before you order a digital certificate

You should check that a digital certificate with an electronic Companies House signature will be accepted by:

  • the person or organisation asking for the certificate (for example, the bank or embassy)
  • your notary, if you are getting the certificate notarised

If they need a paper certificate with a wet ink signature, you should order a paper certificate instead.

Digital certificates do not include a statement of good standing. If you need a statement of good standing, you should order a paper certificate instead.
