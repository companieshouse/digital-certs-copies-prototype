Order a certificate

Use this service to order a paper or digital signed certificate of incorporation for a company, including all company name changes.

You can add certain information to a paper certificate, including:

  • a statement of good standing
  • registered office address
  • directors
  • secretaries
  • company objects

Digital certificates will include all information, except a statement of good standing.

This certificate is different to the incorporation documents that were issued when the company was formed. Copies of these can be ordered through the Order a certified document service.

Choosing paper or digital certificates

You can choose paper and digital certificates for most orders.

You may need to order a paper certificate if the person who is asking for it needs:

  • to include a statement of good standing
  • a paper copy with a wet ink signature
