What information will be included on paper and digital certificates?

Digital certificates will include:

  • company name and number
  • incorporation date
  • current registered office address
  • directors
  • secretaries
  • company objects

You cannot order a digital certificate with a statement of good standing.

Paper certificates will include the company name and number. You can add information, including:

  • a statement of good standing
  • incorporation date
  • current and previous registered office address
  • directors
  • secretaries
  • company objects
