Order a certified document

Use this service to order a paper or digital certified copy of a document in a company's filing history.

You'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • a debit or credit card

You can choose to order digital and paper copies of certified documents. You should contact the person asking for the document to check what they need before ordering.

How are paper and digital documents certified?
Paper copies will be signed in person by one of our officials. Digital copies will be a PDF document, signed with an approved Companies House electronic signature.

To order for a different company, search for the company on the register.

Ordering incorporation documents

Orders for incorporation documents consist of the certificate of incorporation and all other documents from when the company was formed.

You cannot use this service to order a certificate that confirms company details and can include a statement of good standing (known as a certificate of good standing). For this, you'll need to use our Order a certificate service

Ordering a certified document takes around 5 minutes.
