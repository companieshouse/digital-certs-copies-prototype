Order a certified document

Use this service to order a certified copy of a document in a company's filing history.

You'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • a debit or credit card

Ordering incorporation documents

Orders for incorporation documents consist of the certificate of incorporation and all other documents from when the company was formed.

You cannot use this service to order a certificate that confirms company details and can include a statement of good standing (known as a certificate of good standing). For this, you'll need to use our Order a certificate service

Ordering a certified document takes around 5 minutes.
