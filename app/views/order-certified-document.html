THE WOOL COMPANY

Order a certified document

Use this service to order a paper or digital signed document for a company.

You can also use this service to order an incorporation package, which includes the documents that were issued when the company was formed.

To order a certified document or an incorporation package, you'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • a debit or credit card for payment

To order for a different company, search for the company on the register.

Before you start

Check you're using the right service

You can use this service to order a certified copy of a document or incorporation package from the company’s filing history.

You cannot use this service to order a certificate that confirms company details and can include a statement of good standing. For this, you'll need to use our Order a certificate service.

Check the document you need to order

You should check the company’s filing history to:

  • find and preview the document you need to order
  • make sure the document is available - for some older documents you may need to order a scan of the original document first

If you don’t need a certified copy, you can print or save a copy of the document from the filing history.

Choosing paper or digital documents

You can choose to order digital and paper copies of certified documents. You may need to order a paper document if the person who is asking for it needs a paper copy with a wet ink signature.

How are paper and digital documents certified?

Paper copies will be signed with a blue, wet ink signature by one of our officials.

Digital copies will be a PDF document, embedded with an approved Registrar of Companies electronic signature. You will need to open the PDF in Adobe Reader to view the signature.

An electronic signature will not look like a wet ink signature. It will show the Companies House seal and the date that the document was embedded with the electronic signature.
