How to order

You’ll need to use the Find and update company information service to search for the company you want to order certified documents or certificates for. Once you've found and selected the company, you'll need to:

  • go to the company overview page
  • select the ‘More’ tab

You can also order by calling the Companies House contact centre on 0303 1234 500.

Ordering a company certificate

A company certificate includes certified information about a company. The information you can include will depend on whether you order a paper or digital certificate.

You can only order digital certificates for limited companies that have:

  • been incorporated under the Companies Act 2006
  • been in continuous, unbroken existence since incorporation
  • not re-registered to a different company type
  • not changed their name

What information can be included on paper certificates

A paper certificate will include the company’s name, company number and the incorporation date. You can request to add company information, including:

  • a statement of good standing
  • current and previous registered office address
  • current directors
  • current secretaries
  • nature of business
  • company objects
What is a statement of good standing?

The good standing wording says that a company has been in continuous, unbroken existence since its incorporation and that no action is currently being taken to strike the company off the register.

We’ll only issue a good standing statement if the company is up to date with its filings. It must be a:

  • private limited company with at least one director who’s a natural person
  • public limited company with a secretary and at least 2 directors (one must be a natural person)

You cannot order certificates with information on stakeholders shareholdings or statement of capital.

What information is included on digital certificates

For now, digital certificates will include set company information and cannot be customised. They will not include a statement of good standing.

A digital certificate will include the:

  • company name and number
  • incorporation date
  • current registered office address
  • nature of business

Ordering a certified document or incorporation package

Use the Order a certified document service to order a paper or digital:

  • certified copy of a document from the company’s filing history, or
  • incorporation package, which includes all the documents from when a company was formed

Before you order, you should check the company’s filing history to make sure the document you need is available. For some older documents you may need to order a scan of the original document first.

Choosing paper or digital

Paper certificates and documents will be signed with a blue, wet ink signature by one of our officials.

Digital certificates and documents will be a PDF document, embedded with an approved electronic signature. These are only valid in digital form and can be shared by email.

You will need to open the files in Adobe Reader to view the signature. You can download Adobe Reader for free.

You should contact the person asking for the certificate or document to check what they need before ordering.

Legalise a document or certificate

You can get a document legalised with the Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office (FCDO). In countries who have signed up to the Hague Convention of 1961, this gives the document the same authenticity it has in the UK.

To get a Companies House document legalised by the FCDO, it must have the original signature of a British public official, or an embedded electronic Companies House Signature.
