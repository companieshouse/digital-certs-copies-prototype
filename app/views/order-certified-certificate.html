Order a company certificate

Use this service to order a paper or digital signed certificate for a company.

You'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • a debit or credit card

To order for a different company, search for the company on the register.

Before you start

Check that you’re using the right service. Use this service to order a company certificate with certified information about the company.

A certificate can be used to prove that a company is incorporated, and that no action is being taken to remove the company from the register.

This certificate is different to the incorporation documents that were issued when the company was formed. Copies of these can be ordered through the Order a certified document service.

Choosing paper or digital

Paper certificates will be signed with a blue, wet ink signature by one of our officials.

A paper certificate will include the company name, company number and incorporation date. You can choose to add more information about the company, for example a statement of good standing.

Digital certificates will be a PDF document, embedded with an approved Registrar of Companies electronic signature. You will need to open the PDF in Adobe Reader to view the signature. You can download Adobe Reader for free.

For now, digital certificates will include set company information and cannot be customised. They will not include a statement of good standing. If you need this, you should order a paper certificate instead.

Read the guidance to find out what information can be included on paper and digital certificates.
