What type of certificate do you need?

Digital

A digital certificate is a PDF document, embedded with an approved electronic signature.

It cannot be customised and will include set information.

A digital certificate will include:

  • company name and number
  • incorporation date
  • current registered office address
  • current directors
  • current secretaries
  • nature of business
A digital certificate will not include a statement of good standing.

Cost and dispatch

Digital: £9

Your document will be available to download from Your orders within 1 hour. It will be available to download for XX days.

{{ govukButton({ text: "Choose and continue" }) }}

Paper

A paper certificate will be signed with a blue, wet ink signature by one of our officials.

It will include basic company information and you can choose to add more information if you need to.

A paper certificate will include:

  • company name and number
  • incorporation date

You can customise a paper certificate by adding:

  • Statement of good standing
  • current and previous registered office address
  • current and previous directors
  • current and previous secretaries
  • company objects

Cost and dispatch

Standard dispatch: £15

We will aim to send out the certificate within 10 working days.

Express dispatch: £50

Orders received before 11am will be sent out the same day and orders received after 11am the next working day.
