Before you order a digital document

You should check that a digital document with an electronic signature will be accepted by:

  • the person or organisation asking for the document (for example, the bank or embassy)
  • your notary, if you are getting the document notarised

The document will only be valid in digital form as it contains a unique electronic signature. If they need a paper document with a wet ink signature, you should order a paper document instead.

What is an electronic signature?

An electronic signature will not look like a wet ink signature. It will show the Companies House seal and the date that the document was embedded with the electronic signature.

You will need to open the PDF in Adobe reader to view the signature.
