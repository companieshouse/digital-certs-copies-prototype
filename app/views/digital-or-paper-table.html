What type of certificate do you need?

Certified certificates are available in digital or paper format.

A digital certificate is a PDF document, embedded with an approved electronic signature. It cannot be customised and will include set information.

A paper certificate will be signed with a blue, wet ink signature by one of our officials. It will include basic company information and you can choose to add more information if you need to.
